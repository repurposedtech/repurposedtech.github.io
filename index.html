Repurposed Hard Drive Clock

This repurposed hard drive clock is a real find for a techie admirer and unusual home decor lover. It is a great conversation starter for anyone who sees this awesome clock!

The table/desk clock is made of a recycled computer hard drive. The clock has quartz movement, hour, minute and second clock hands. Clock runs on AA battery.

The measurement of hard drive clock is 4 " x 6"

Choose from two types of clocks:
1) Keys F12,F3,F9: Hour, minute, second clock hands with Keyboard keys F12, F3 & F9 mounted on metal hard drive disk
2) No Keys: Hour, minute, second clock hands.
